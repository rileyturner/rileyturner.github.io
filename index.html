A little about me...

Hey! my name's Riley! I'm a 20-year-old student studying computer science at the University of Adelaide, majoring in artificial intelligence.

In 2021, I had my first hands-on experience with programming, in the form of a 2D side-scrolling game made within Unity. While the project faced some challenges, it sparked a deep interest in game devlopment and software engineering as a career pathway.

In 2023, I began my bachelor's degree in computer science, majoring in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This degree was my first real introduction to computer science concepts like cyber security, object-oriented programming, and software engineering. By the end of the year, I had developed a 2D RPG-style game within C++, and blackjack within MATLAB.

By 2024, I had deepened my understanding of computer internals down to the chip level, as well as expanded my knowledge in relation to the production and hosting of both static and dynamic web pages. This page is a testament to said understanding.

In 2025, I began to take courses that were far more specific to my major, specifically that of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and cyber security. With some free time on my hands, I began to work on some small passion projects such as a Discord bot and an automated Twitch engagement bot. Both projects are covered within the Projects section.

In my free time I enjoy playing hero shooters, watching Netflix, and tackling coding problems. I am highly competitive by nature, and I believe this drive pushes me to continually improve - not just in my skills, but in my character wherever possible.
