A little about me...

From a young age, I've always been fascinated with computers. This hidden magic that allowed me to play my favourite games, watch digitalised content, and communicate with the greater digital community.

The idea of automation has always interested me, from placing rocks on my keyboard to automate keyboard inputs, to creating legitimate applications capable of functioning regardless of what the user was doing, I wanted to learn more.

In 2021, I had my first experience with Unity, in the form of a 2D side scrolling game. The application didn't turn out as I had hoped, largely as a result of my limited programming experience, however this only served to fuel my dream of software development as a career opportunity.

In 2023, I started my bachelor's degree in computer science, majoring in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This degree was my first real introduction to the vastly intriguing field, introducing me to a plethora of exciting computer science topics, such as memory management, object-oriented programming and software engineering. Through these courses I developed a 2D RPG-style game within C++, and blackjack within MATLAB using ASCII art.

In 2024, I began to refine my understanding of a computer's internal functions down to the chip level, as well as expand my knowledge in relation to the production and hosting of both static and dynamic web pages. This page serves to demonstrate said understanding.

In my free time I enjoy playing FPS shooter games, watching Netflix, and solving coding problems. I am a very competitive person and strive to improve and refine my character in all aspects wherever possible.
