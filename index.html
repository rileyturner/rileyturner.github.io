A little about me...

I've always been fascinated with computers. This hidden magic that allowed me to play my favourite games, watch digitalised content, and communicate with the greater digital community. From a young age, the idea of automation has always captivated me, whether it was placing rocks on my keyboard to automate keyboard inputs or creating legitimate applications capable of functioning regardless of what the user was doing. I wanted to learn more.

In 2021, I had my first hands-on experience with programming, in the form of a 2D side-scrolling game made within Unity. Although the project didn't turn out as I had hoped, it served to fuel my dream of software development as a career pathway.

In 2023, I began my bachelor's degree in computer science, majoring in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This degree was my first real introduction to the vastly intriguing field, introducing me to a fascinating array of computer science concepts, such as memory management, object-oriented programming, and software engineering. Through these courses, I developed a 2D RPG-style game within C++, and blackjack within MATLAB using ASCII art.

By 2024, I had deepened my understanding of computer internals down to the chip level, as well as expanded my knowledge in relation to the production and hosting of both static and dynamic web pages. This page is a testament to said understanding.

In my free time I enjoy playing FPS shooter games, watching Netflix, and tackling coding problems. I am a very competitive person and strive to improve and refine my character in all aspects wherever possible.
